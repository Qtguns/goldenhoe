About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game that challenges players to survive waves of monsters for as long as possible. Inspired by popular roguelike and survival games, Monster Survivors combines fast-paced action with strategic gameplay.

In Monster Survivors, you'll face increasingly difficult waves of monsters while collecting experience points to level up and unlock new abilities. Choose your character wisely, as each has unique strengths and weaknesses that can dramatically change your playstyle.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. Survive as long as possible while defeating as many monsters as you can!

Game Tips

  • Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters
  • Prioritize leveling up over collecting gold in the early game
  • Choose abilities that complement your character's strengths
  • Don't be afraid to retreat if you're overwhelmed
  • Area damage abilities are essential for dealing with large groups
